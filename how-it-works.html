How it works

  1. Free consultation — Share your goals via call or WhatsApp.
  2. Assessment — Property inspection or investment evaluation and plan.
  3. Agreement & onboarding — Transparent scope, pricing, and timelines.
  4. Execution — Listing/marketing, tenant management, maintenance, or advisory.
  5. Updates & reports — Monthly/quarterly status, rent, and maintenance logs.
  6. Closure or ongoing support — One‑time closings or continuous management.

Technology keeps you informed: digital records, rent tracking, remote video walkthroughs, WhatsApp & email support.
